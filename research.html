NeuroInsight: Automated Hippocampal Volumetric Analysis for Clinical Research

Validated methodology for T1-weighted MRI processing and asymmetry detection. This comprehensive study demonstrates the clinical accuracy and reliability of NeuroInsight's automated hippocampal segmentation compared to manual expert annotations.

Key Findings

  • • 95% agreement with expert segmentation
  • • Processing time reduced by 80%
  • • Consistent asymmetry calculations
  • • HIPAA-compliant local processing

Methodology

  • • FreeSurfer 7.4.1 integration
  • • Automated recon-all pipeline
  • • Statistical validation framework
  • • Clinical cohort validation
